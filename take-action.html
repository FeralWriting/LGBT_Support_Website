Issues and Struggles Facing the Community Today

  • In the US, almost one fifth of hate crimes are committed because of the victim's LGBT+ status.
  • Only 24 states have protections for LGBT+ people being discriminated against when adopting, 11 states actively allow discrimination to occur.
  • Conversion therapy, a form of abuse, is not banned in 41 states and leads to increased health risk among LGBT+ teens.
  • 28 states allow firing of LGBT+ individuals solely on their status as part of the community.
  • 25 states allow housing discrimination against LGBT+ individuals.
  • Only 18 states have protections against bathroom discrimination for LGBT+ individuals.
  • Doctors show a severe bias against LGBT+ individuals.
  • The justice system disproportionately affects LGBT+ individuals.
  • Young LGBT+ individuals are 1.2x more likely to be homeless than their non-LGBT+ counterparts.
